Eat The Pain


TIMEFRAME: 3 days, part-time
ENGINE: Twine 2.0

Inspired by Amanda Palmer's quote, "Eat the pain. Send it back into the void as love", I created the original version of this game in 3 hour period of despair on the day Trump was declared president. Made partially to distract myself, I wanted to create something that could act as stress relief while also providing some hope.

In the game, the player simply pops nine ‘bad’ symbols and turns them into hearts and stars. Once all nine have been popped, they are rewarded with a quote. The design influence behind this was bubble wrap and how therapeutic it is when you are stressed.

I originally created the game in Unreal but then rebuilt it in Twine 2.0 as it allowed me create a web build and add quotes more easily. Being a solo project, I curated the colours and fonts as well as scripting the functionality and hover effects.

ALIGHT


ROLE: Creative Director and Artist
TIMEFRAME: 5 weeks, part-time
TEAM SIZE: 6 people
ENGINE: Unity 4.5

Alight was a student project which was created for an assignment that stipulated that all characters must be identical. From these requirements, I came up with a simple game about leading a group of fireflies through a dark forest. When moving through the environment, the trees would react to the light of the group.

My role for this project was Creative Director and Artist. I determined the overall visual style and also created the visual assets for the game. Working closely with Sam McLean, our audio guy, I worked to make a cohesive and stylish environment and game.

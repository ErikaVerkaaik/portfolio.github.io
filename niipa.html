NIIPA


ROLE: Creative director & lead artist
TIMEFRAME: 6 months, part-time
TEAM SIZE: 4 people
ENGINE: Unreal 4.12

This project was a world building exercise: we wanted to create an intriguing environment and character that felt like it was part of a much larger world.

Niipa is a simple, exploratory game about a 12 year old goatherd who lives on a set of floating islands. The game allows you to walk in her footsteps, explore her home and uncover the story of how she came to live amongst the clouds.

My role in this project was that of creative director and lead artist. Having come up with the original concept for the game, I was responsible for the visual design and creation of 3D assets. I also worked with the rest of the team to ensure that the gameplay and audio suited the aesthetic of the game.

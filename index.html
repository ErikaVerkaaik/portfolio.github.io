Erika Verkaaik

Hi, my name is Erika and I'm a Brisbane based game developer. Known for creating trash games and exploratory digital spaces, I like to experiment and create small, weird things. I am extremely passionate about tiny, experimental games and run a blog on which I curate and analyse such games. In my spare time, I also organise and run a local game dev meetup called Squiggly River.

Contact me at: erikaverkaaik@gmail.com or via Twitter.

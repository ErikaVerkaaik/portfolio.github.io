Reliquary


STATUS: Work in progress, on-going
ENGINE: Unreal 4.13

Reliquary is a game about rituals and relics. In this game, the player wanders around a set of geometric ruins. Their movement and interaction triggers in-world events and brings the ruins “back to life”.

The original concept came from a small prototype, called Beautiful, which I made for a 7 day game jam. In this game the player wandered a wasteland and their movement could trigger certain objects to come to life. I want to flesh out this concept with more planned out art, a higher level of interaction and an actual end goal.

Inspired by the work of French artist Céleste Boursier-Mougenot (especially his ‘From here to ear’ series) I want to create a space that allows for exploration and which organically responds to the viewer. The subtle and organic reactions in his work are familiar and understandable to the viewer; thus creating a space that is believable and autonomous from our own. I really want to capture that sense of a grounded world despite the abstract nature of my art and setting.
